DSVV Mentorship Program

Empowering Alumni and Students to Grow Together

About the Program

Our mentorship program connects current students with experienced alumni to foster personal and professional development. It's an opportunity to learn, guide, and grow through meaningful connections.

Why Join?

For Students (Mentees)

  • Career guidance from industry professionals
  • Skill development and networking
  • Personal growth and confidence

For Alumni (Mentors)

  • Give back to the community
  • Shape future leaders
  • Refine leadership and communication skills

How It Works

The program runs in 3-month cycles. Mentors and mentees are matched based on interests, career goals, and experience. Weekly or bi-weekly check-ins are encouraged, with support from the alumni office throughout.
