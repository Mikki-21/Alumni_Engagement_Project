About DSVVAE

Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya (DSVV) is a premier institution located in the serene environment of Haridwar, Uttarakhand, founded with the vision of revitalizing Indian culture and values through modern education. Established under the guidance of Pt. Shriram Sharma Acharya, the founder of the All World Gayatri Pariwar, DSVV aims to create a new generation of youth who are intellectually sound, emotionally balanced, and spiritually awakened.

The university stands out for its unique curriculum that integrates academic excellence with practical training in life skills, ethics, and social responsibility. It offers various programs in disciplines such as Yogic Science, Indian Culture, Clinical Psychology, Journalism and Mass Communication, Education, Computer Science, and Holistic Health.

DSVV promotes a lifestyle rooted in Indian traditions like yoga, meditation, and value- based living. The university campus is designed to foster inner peace, discipline, and selfless service. Students are encouraged to participate in community service, environmental conservation, and research with a strong emphasis on sustainable development and human values.

With national and international collaborations, DSVV has gained global recognition as a center for spiritual education, cultural exchange, and transformative learning. It aspires to be a lighthouse of Indian wisdom for the entire world, blending the best of ancient heritage and modern knowledge.
